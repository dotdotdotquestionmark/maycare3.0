About Us

Palo Alto Care Homes has served the Bay Area for nearly 25 years. With locations in Palo Alto and Los Altos, we provide senior care for both ambulatory and non-ambulatory guests in a lovely residential setting. Our homes were founded in 1987 by Mrs. May Wang and are now operated by her son James. Our caring and professional staff – some of whom have been with us for over 15 years – operate a warm and cheerful environment where your loved ones will feel at home. We ensure that our residents always cared for by having a minimum of two staff members on duty 24/7. We are grateful to be entrusted with the caring and well-being of your loved ones and we work hard to make them feel at home and provide them with a peaceful environment with friendly and dependable care.
