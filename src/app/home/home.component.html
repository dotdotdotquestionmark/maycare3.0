Serving Bay Area Seniors and their families since 1987

Family Run Senior Care Homes

Palo Alto Care Homes has served the Bay Area since 1987. We have two senior care homes: one in Palo Alto and a second in Los Altos. Both of our homes are staffed by trained, caring and experienced professionals.

At Palo Alto Care Homes, our residents are part of one big family and when they are with us, we treat them as if they are are part of our very own family.

Our Philosophy

We believe that the basic principle of longevity lies in the harmony and balance of mental and physical health. Peace of mind and a sense of community will keep the elderly young at heart and free from worries. We aim to provide a friendly, caring environment that offers harmony, comfort and security and allows residents to live as they wish.
